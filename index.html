ABOUT NISAANMOTOR

WE ARE A TRUSTED NAME IN AUTO INDUSTRY

VISITED BY MILLION OF CAR BUYERS EVERY MONTH!

Nisaan Motor Co., Ltd. is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Nishi-ku, Yokohama, Japan. The company sells its vehicles under the Nisaan, Infiniti, and Datsun brands, with in-house performance tuning products labelled Nismo.


Nisaan's first final assembly robots were installed in the Murayama plant (where the then-new March/Micra was assembled) in 1982. In 1984 the Zama plant began to be robotized; this automation process then continued throughout Nisaan's factories.
